ğŸ›‚ Visa Requirements & Moving to Canada

Study Permit Application

Most international students need a study permit for programs longer than 6 months. Apply as soon as you receive your letter of acceptance.

Required Documents

  • Letter of acceptance from designated institution
  • Valid passport
  • Proof of financial support
  • Biometrics and medical exam (if required)

Helpful Resources:

  • Apply for Study Permit

Renewing Your Permit

Apply at least 30 days before expiration. You'll need confirmation of enrollment and proof of financial support.

ğŸ“ Life in Canada as a Student

Campus Life

Join student clubs, use campus facilities, and participate in events and international student services.

Accommodation

  • On-Campus Dorms: $3,000-$7,500 per semester
  • Off-Campus Rentals: $500-$1,500+ per month
  • Homestay: $700-$1,200 per month with meals

Cost of Living

  • Rent: $500-$1,500
  • Food: $200-$400
  • Transportation: $80-$150
  • Total: $1,000-$2,500+ per month

Working in Canada

International students can work up to 20 hours per week during semesters and full-time during breaks.

Essential Services

  • Open a Canadian bank account
  • Get a Social Insurance Number (SIN) if working
  • Arrange health insurance
  • Get a phone plan

Helpful Resources:

  • Social Insurance Number
  • Settlement.org
